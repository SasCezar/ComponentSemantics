2 Page Spread viewing (two images once, for digital books/comics)\n - To read archives directly (zip/7z/rar/cbz/cb7/cbr/cab/tar.gz/tar.bz2/tar.xz/etc...)\n - To show images (bmp/png/jpeg/ico/dds/tga/tiff/gif)\n   - Supported for modern formats(JPEG 2000/JPEG XR/WebP)\n   - Supported for Animated PNG(APNG)/GIF\n   - Auto rotation (using JPEG EXIF)\n - To show Raw digital camera images\n   - Canon(crw, cr2)\n   - Nicon(nef)\n   - Sony(arw)\n   - Ricoh(dng)\n   - etc..\n - Can make/view/search catalog from thousands of your digital books\n - Multilingual support(English/Spanish/Simplified Chinese/French/Russian/Japanese/Greek)\n\n## Download\n\n[binary distributions](https://github.com/kanryu/quickviewer/releases)\n\n - Create shortcut, and drop image folder/archive on it.\n\n\n## Features\n\n- Next Page (Press 'right Arrow Key', 'Mouse wheel down', 'Mouse forward button', or Click left end of window)\n- Prev Page (Press 'left Arrow Key', 'Mouse wheel up', 'Mouse back button', or Click right end of window)\n- Fullscreen (Press 'F11 Key', click Mouse wheel, or 'Esc Key'(to windowed only))\n- Auto loading (auto loading the last book when the app executed)\n- Fittng (To fit images up to window size)\n- Spread viewing (two image once)\n    - Bound on the right side book(e.g. Japanese Style)\n- Pre page loading(since v0.2.0)\n- Exif Information(since v0.3.0)\n- Show Windows Explorer for the current image\n- Put the current image into your mailer\n- Shader Program Filters(since v0.5.1)\n    - Nearest Neighber\n    - Bilinear\n    - ~~Bicubic (by GPU, obsoleted since v0.9.3)~~\n    - ~~Lanczos (obsoleted since v0.9.3)~~\n    - Bicubic by CPU\n    - Lanczos3 by CPU (since 1.0.9)\n    - Lanczos4 by CPU (since 1.0.9)\n- Catalog(since v0.6.0)\n    - Database for your digital books, can manage thansands of volumes, quick view and search\n- File Associations(since v0.8.3)\n- Retouching(change Brightness, Contrast, Gamma value, since 1.0.8)\n\n## Screen operations\n\nYou can do screen operations with the main menu, tool bar, mouse button, wheel, keyboard and so on.\n\n| Operation\xc2\xa0  |
