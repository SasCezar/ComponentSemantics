b"[![Coding time tracker](https://wakatime.com/badge/github/wakatime/atom-wakatime.svg)](https://wakatime.com/badge/github/wakatime/atom-wakatime)\n\n# atom-wakatime\n\nQuantify your coding inside GitHub's Atom editor.\n\n## Installation\n\n![install](./install.gif)\n\n1. Inside Atom, navigate to `Preferences`/`Settings` \xe2\x86\x92 `Install` and search for `wakatime`.\n\n  ![Project Overview](https://wakatime.com/static/img/ScreenShots/atom-wakatime-install-1.png)\n\n2. Click the `Install` button.\n\n  ![Project Overview](https://wakatime.com/static/img/ScreenShots/atom-wakatime-install-2.png)\n\n3. Click `Settings`, then enter your [api key](https://wakatime.com/settings#apikey).\n\n4. Use Atom and your coding activity will be displayed on your [WakaTime dashboard](https://wakatime.com).\n\nNote: The leet way to install WakaTime is with this Terminal command:\n\n    apm install wakatime\n\n\n## Screen Shots\n\n![Project Overview](https://wakatime.com/static/img/ScreenShots/Screen-Shot-2016-03-21.png)\n\n## Configuring\n\nSettings for atom-wakatime are under `Settings \xe2\x86\x92 Packages \xe2\x86\x92 wakatime \xe2\x86\x92 Settings`.\n\nAdditional settings are in `$HOME/.wakatime.cfg` for [wakatime cli](https://github.com/wakatime/wakatime#configuring).\n\n## Troubleshooting\n\nAtom writes errors to the Atom Developer Console. To check for errors:\n\n1. Turn on the debug checkbox in `Settings \xe2\x86\x92 Packages \xe2\x86\x92 wakatime \xe2\x86\x92 Settings`\n2. Inside Atom, go to `View \xe2\x86\x92 Developer \xe2\x86\x92 Toggle Developer Tools`\n3. Clear the developer console (the circle button top left of the console)\n4. Inside Atom, go to `View \xe2\x86\x92 Developer \xe2\x86\x92 Reload Window`\n\nThat will reload all plugins including WakaTime, and you should see the startup process logged in the developer console.\nIf there are no errors in your developer console after startup and editing a file, then check for errors in your `~/.wakatime.log` file.\n\nFor checking your `~/.wakatime.log` file, see [wakatime/wakatime#troubleshooting](https://github.com/wakatime/wakatime#troubleshooting).\n\nNote: Behind a proxy? Configure Atom to use your proxy when installing wakatime: <https://github.com/atom/apm#behind-a-firewall>\n"
