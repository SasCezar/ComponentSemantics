Dimens\n\n**Pattern:**\n```\nR.dimen.material_[layout|component|typography]_[keylines|button|...]\n\nR.color.material_[red|blue|...]_[500|800|...]\n```\n \n## Usage\nJust add the dependency to your `build.gradle`:\n```\ncompile \'com.dmitrymalkovich.android:material-design-dimens:1.4\'\n```\n\n## Examples\n\n#### Dialogs\n\n<img width="600"
