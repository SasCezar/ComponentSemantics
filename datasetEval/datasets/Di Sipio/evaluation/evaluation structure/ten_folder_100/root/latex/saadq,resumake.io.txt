You can change your template at any point to see how your resume looks with different designs.\n\nWhen you\'re happy with your result, you can download the resume as a PDF, TeX, or JSON document. The JSON output is compatible with [JSONResume](https://jsonresume.org).\n\n## Contributing\nContributions are very welcome, and I am always happy to help out first-timers contributors with any questions you may have.
