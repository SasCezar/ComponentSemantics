b'# ![logo](https://raw.githubusercontent.com/sagemathinc/smc/master/src/webapp-lib/favicon-32x32.png) CoCalc\n\n#### _Collaborative Calculation in the Cloud_\n\n**CoCalc** offers collaborative calculation in the cloud.\nThis includes working with the full (scientific) Python stack, SageMath, Julia, R, Octave, and more.\nIt also offers capabilities to author documents in LaTeX, R/knitr or Markdown,\nstoring and organizing files, a web-based Linux Terminal,\ncommunication tools like a chat, course management and more.\n\n## Website\n\n   * **[CoCalc](https://cocalc.com) -- the online service**\n   * [Github](https://github.com/sagemathinc/cocalc)\n   * [Mailing List](https://groups.google.com/forum/#!forum/cocalc)\n\n## Very easy install of CoCalc on your computer\n\nYou can easily use CoCalc on your own computer for free by **[running a Docker image](https://github.com/sagemathinc/cocalc-docker)**.\n\n## History\n\n*CoCalc* was formerly called *SageMathCloud*.\nIt started to offer way more than just SageMath and hence outgrew itself.\nThe name was coined in fall 2016 and changed around spring 2017.\n\n## Contributors\n\n### Current highly active contributors\n\n   * John Jeng\n   * Harald Schilly\n   * Hal Snyder\n   * William Stein\n   * Travis Scholl\n\n### Past contributors\n\n   * Greg Bard\n   * Rob Beezer\n   * Keith Clawson\n   * Tim Clemans\n   * Andy Huchala\n   * Jon Lee\n   * Simon Luu\n   * Nicholas Ruhland\n   * Todd Zimmerman\n\n... and *many* others: See https://github.com/sagemathinc/cocalc/graphs/contributors\n\n## Copyright/License\n\nThe copyright of CoCalc is owned by SageMath, Inc., and the source code\nhere is released under the GNU Affero General Public License version 3+.\nSee the included file LICENSE.md.\n\nNone of the frontend or server dependencies of SMC are themselves GPL\nlicensed; they all have non-viral liberal licenses.
