b'<p align="center">\n    <img src="https://i.imgur.com/Kd7N6uf.png">\n</p>\n\n---\n\nPlotSquared is a land and world management plugin for Minecraft. \nIt includes several highly configurable world generators. \nYou can create plots of land in existing worlds using plot clusters, or you can have a full world of plots.\n\nFor the end user, PlotSquared is packed with a tonne of cool features.\nIt allows you to merge plots, and build together with your friends. \nYou can also change a lot of plot specific settings in the form of\nflags. Such as: weather, time, game modes, pvp status. \n\nWhilst we provide a whole load of unique features, the biggest focus\nis to provide a lag-free and smooth experience.\n\n***NOTE:** This is the branch for the 1.13+ version of PlotSquared.
