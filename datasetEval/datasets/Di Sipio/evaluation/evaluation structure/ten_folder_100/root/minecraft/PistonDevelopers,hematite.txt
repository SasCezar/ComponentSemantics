b'# hematite [![Build Status](https://travis-ci.org/PistonDevelopers/hematite.svg?branch=master)](https://travis-ci.org/PistonDevelopers/hematite)\n\nA simple Minecraft written in Rust with the Piston game engine\n\n![screenshot](./screenshot.png)\n\n## How To Open a World\n\n*This method is only for personal use. Never distribute copyrighted content from Minecraft.*\n\n* In the Minecraft Launcher, click the button \xe2\x80\x9cNew Profile\xe2\x80\x9d\n* In the drop down "use version", select `1.8.8`\n* Click \xe2\x80\x9cSave Profile\xe2\x80\x9d\n* Click \xe2\x80\x9cPlay\xe2\x80\x9d (this will download the snapshot)\n* Quit Minecraft\n\n* **Copy** your world save to to the hematite directory (It may corrupt your world)\n* Save Locations:\n  * **Windows:** `%appdata%\\.minecraft\\saves\\`\n  * **OSX:** `~/Library/Application Support/minecraft/saves/`\n  * **Linux/Other:** `~/.minecraft/saves/`\n* Run hematite with: `cargo run --release "./<WORLD_NAME>"`\n\n## Dependencies\n\n![dependencies](./Cargo.png)\n\n[How to contribute](https://github.com/PistonDevelopers/piston/blob/master/CONTRIBUTING.md)\n'
