Right-click the dock/tray icon and choose `Quit` to completely quit the app. On macOS, click the dock icon to show the window. On Linux, right-click the tray icon and choose `Toggle` to toggle the window. On Windows, click the tray icon to toggle the window.\n\n### Keyboard shortcuts\n\n- New tweet: <kbd>n</kbd>\n- New DM: <kbd>m</kbd>\n- Send tweet/DM: <kbd>Cmd</kbd> <kbd>Enter</kbd> or
