b'![Movie Monad](https://i.imgur.com/gdsyIMv.png)\n\n## Is Movie Monad the free video player I\'ve been looking for?\n\nYes. :heavy_check_mark:  \n\nMovie Monad is a free and simple to use video player made with Haskell.\nOriginally it was a proof of concept to add video playback to\n[Gifcurry](https://lettier.github.io/gifcurry/) (another great app\xe2\x80\x94check it out).\nNowadays it\'s a lightweight yet mighty media player used all over the\n[world](https://snapcraft.io/movie-monad#js-snap-map)\n:globe_with_meridians: everyday.\n\nMovie Monad is cross platform so you\'ll never have to watch without it.\nStream videos from the web or play the files stored on your computer.\nPlay, pause, seek, repeat, resize, expand, and turn it up\xe2\x80\x94Movie Monad gets out of\nthe way so you can watch the videos you love. :heartbeat:\n\nSo try out Movie Monad\xe2\x80\x94it\'ll make your life simpler. :sunglasses:\n\n## What does Movie Monad look like? :eyes:\n\n![Movie Monad](https://i.imgur.com/r1btBMF.gif)\n\n## What can I do with Movie Monad? :thinking:\n\n* You can play video files from your hard drive :computer: or stream videos from the web. :spider_web:\n    * To play web videos, click the file open button, paste the URL into the text box, and click open.\n    * Movie Monad can handle `file://`, `https://`, and `http://`.\n* You can play and pause. :play_or_pause_button:\n* You can seek/scrub :rewind: :fast_forward: through the video. :vhs:\n* You can put the video on repeat. :repeat:\n* You can slow down :snail: or speed up :racehorse: the video (great for lectures).\n* You can turn subtitles off or on and pick your language.\n* You can turn up :arrow_up:, turn down :arrow_down:, or mute :mute: the volume. :sound:\n* You can expand the video to full screen.\n* You can quickly resize the video to standard sizes.\n* You can resize the video to a custom size by resizing the window.\n    * Movie Monad will responsively adjust the video size to fill the window. :boom:\n* You can play videos from the command line. :cl:\n    * `movie-monad ./path/to/video/file.webm`\n    * `movie-monad http://www.domain.tld/path/to/video/file.webm`\n\n## What are the keyboard controls? :keyboard:\n\n* Mute/Unmute\n    * `m`\n    * `AudioMute`\n* Full Screen/Windowed\n    * `f`\n* Show Controls\n    * `c`\n* Toggle Repeat\n    * `r`\n* Volume Up\n    * `ArrowUp`\n    * `AudioRaiseVolume`\n* Volume Down\n    * `ArrowDown`\n    * `AudioLowerVolume`\n* Seek Left\n    * `ArrowLeft`\n* Seek Right\n    * `ArrowRight`\n* Play/Pause\n    * `Space`\n    * `AudioPlay`\n\n## How do I get a copy of Movie Monad? :floppy_disk:\n\nMovie Monad works on Linux, Mac, and most likely Windows.\nMake sure you have GStreamer and GTK+ installed on your machine.\nTo find the latest version of Movie Monad, head over to the\n[releases page](https://github.com/lettier/movie-monad/releases).\n\n### I use Linux. :penguin:\n\nIf you use Linux then the easiest way to grab a copy of Movie Monad is by downloading the\n[AppImage](https://github.com/lettier/movie-monad/releases/download/0.0.7.0/movie-monad-0.0.7.0-x86_64.AppImage).\nAfter you download the\n[AppImage](https://github.com/lettier/movie-monad/releases/download/0.0.7.0/movie-monad-0.0.7.0-x86_64.AppImage),\nright click on it, select permissions, and check the box near execute.\nWith that out of the way\xe2\x80\x94you\'re all set\xe2\x80\x94just double click on the AppImage\nand Movie Monad will pop up.\n\nYou can also download and install the\n[AppImage](https://github.com/lettier/movie-monad/releases/download/0.0.7.0/movie-monad-0.0.7.0-x86_64.AppImage)\nusing the handy\n[AppImage install script](https://raw.githubusercontent.com/lettier/movie-monad/master/packaging/linux/app-image/movie-monad-install-app-image-script.sh)\n(right click the link and select "Save link as...").\nDownload the script, right click on it, select permissions, check the box near execute, and double click on it.\nYou should now see Movie Monad listed alongside your other installed programs.\n\n#### I use Arch, Manjaro, Antergos, or pacman. :ghost:\n\nYou can install Movie Monad via `pacman`. Copy the following into your terminal.\n\n```bash\ncd "$HOME/Downloads"\nsudo pacman -S git gstreamer gst-plugins-base-libs gst-plugins-base gst-plugins-good gst-plugins-bad gst-libav\ngit clone https://aur.archlinux.org/movie-monad.git\ncd movie-monad\nmakepkg -sic\ncd "$HOME/Downloads"\nrm -rf movie-monad\ncd\nmovie-monad\n```\n\n#### I use Fedora, SUSE, CentOS, Red Hat, Flatpak, Flathub, or GNOME Software. :tophat:\n\nYou can install Movie Monad as a Flatpak via [Flathub](https://flathub.org/apps/details/com.lettier.movie-monad).\n\n```bash\nflatpak remote-add --if-not-exists flathub https://dl.flathub.org/repo/flathub.flatpakrepo\nflatpak install flathub com.lettier.movie-monad\n```\n\nYou can also find it in GNOME Software. Just search for Movie Monad.\n\n#### I use Ubuntu, Mint, Debian, Deepin, or Snap. :cyclone:\n\nMovie Monad is available as a snap from [Snapcraft](https://snapcraft.io/).\nIf you don\'t already have `snap`, go ahead and install it using the command `sudo apt install snapd`.\n\nYou can install the\n[Movie Monad snap](https://snapcraft.io/movie-monad)\nright from your browser or via the command line.\nFor the command line route, paste the following into your terminal.\n\n```bash\nsnap install movie-monad\nsudo snap connect movie-monad:removable-media\nmovie-monad\n```\n\n### I use Mac. :apple:\n\nMac users can download the\n[Mac install script](https://raw.githubusercontent.com/lettier/movie-monad/master/packaging/mac/movie-monad-mac-install-script.command)\nby holding down control, clicking the link,\nselecting "Save Link As...", selecting where Downloads, and clicking save.\nTo run the script, hold down the command key and press the space bar. Now type `terminal` and hit enter.\nAfter the terminal comes up, copy and paste the following.\n\n```bash\ncd ~/Downloads\nchmod +x movie-monad-mac-install-script.command\n./movie-monad-mac-install-script.command\n```\n\nOnce it\'s finished, a shortcut to Movie Monad will be on your desktop.\n\n### I\'m a Haskell developer. :desktop_computer:\n\nIf you develop Haskell programs then the easiest way to build Movie Monad is with\n[Haskell Stack](https://docs.haskellstack.org/en/stable/README/).\nCopy the following into your terminal.\n\n```bash\ncd "$HOME/Downloads"\ngit clone https://github.com/lettier/movie-monad.git\ncd movie-monad\nstack update\nstack setup\nstack install alex happy\nstack install haskell-gi\nstack install\nstack exec -- movie-monad\n```\n\n## What dependencies does Movie Monad use?\n\n### To run Movie Monad.\n\n* [GTK+ >= 3.10](https://www.gtk.org/download/index.php)\n* [GStreamer >= 1.0](https://gstreamer.freedesktop.org/download/)\n    * [GStreamer Base Plug-ins](https://gstreamer.freedesktop.org/modules/gst-plugins-base.html)\n    * [GStreamer Good Plug-ins](https://gstreamer.freedesktop.org/modules/gst-plugins-good.html)\n    * [GStreamer Bad Plug-ins >= 1.8](https://gstreamer.freedesktop.org/modules/gst-plugins-bad.html)\n\n### To build Movie Monad.\n\n* [GObject Introspection](https://wiki.gnome.org/action/show/Projects/GObjectIntrospection)\n* [Haskell Stack](https://docs.haskellstack.org/en/stable/README/)\n\n## What is the license?\n\nSee [LICENSE](LICENSE).\n\n## Who wrote Movie Monad?\n\n(C) 2017 David Lettier  \n[lettier.com](http://www.lettier.com/)\n'
