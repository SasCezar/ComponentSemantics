b'![Gifcurry](https://i.imgur.com/KFZyEqq.png)\n\n# Tell me about Gifcurry. :smiley:\n\nGifcurry is the open-source, Haskell-built video editor for GIF makers.\nLoad a video, make some edits, and save it as a GIF\xe2\x80\x94Gifcurry makes your life easy! :sunglasses:\nMost video formats should work, so go wild.\nAnd since it\'s made with Haskell, you know it\'s good. :100:\n\nFor the command line averse, there is a GUI. :computer_mouse:\nDie-hard terminal aficionado? Gifcurry has you covered with its CLI. :desktop_computer:\nAnd for the Haskell programmers out there, there is also a library :books: API.\n\nGifcurry can save your creation as a GIF or as a video. :vhs:\nSo if you hate :angry: GIFs with a passion\xe2\x80\x94no problem!\nJust select "Save as a Video" and do your part to rid :wastebasket: the world of GIFs.\n\nEnjoy memes? :trollface: Great! Gifcurry can add text all over your GIF.\nYou can change the font, size, color, position, outline, rotation, and the timing.\nCreate the next viral meme with Gifcurry.\n\nDid you know Gifcurry slices...and dices? :fork_and_knife:\nYou can crop :scissors: from the left, the right, the top, and/or the bottom.\nWith Gifcurry, you can slice up some tasty GIFs.\n\nIs Gifcurry another Electron app? No way! Gifcurry is 100% #ElectronFree.\nNo need to download more RAM :ram:, Gifcurry is light as a feather.\nRun :running: it all day, run it all year\xe2\x80\x94you\'ll never notice.\n\n"So...Gifcurry is just FFmpeg and ImageMagick?"\xe2\x80\x94nope.\nGifcurry hides all the goofy details so you can concentrate on what matters\xe2\x80\x94the almighty :raised_hands: GIF.\n\nBecome a GIF master :muscle: with Gifcurry!\n\n## What do I need Gifcurry for? :thinking:\n\n:ballot_box_with_check: Want to show off that new UI feature in a pull request? Height will scale\n                         to match.\n  -f --fps=INT           How many frames per second the output should have.\n  -c --color-count=INT   How many colors are used in the output.\n  -d --dither            If present, uses dither.\nCROP:\n  -L --left-crop=NUM     The amount you wish to crop from the left.\n  -R --right-crop=NUM    The amount you wish to crop from the right.\n  -T --top-crop=NUM      The amount you wish to crop from the top.\n  -B --bottom-crop=NUM   The amount you wish to crop from the bottom.\nTEXT:\n  -t --text-file=FILE    Either a text overlays YAML or SRT subtitles file\n                         path.\n  -? --help              Display help message\n  -V --version           Print version information\n\nVisit https://github.com/lettier/gifcurry for more information.\n```\n\n## Got a CLI example?\n\n```text\ngifcurry_cli \\\n  -i ~/Videos/video.webm \\\n  -o ~/tmp/test \\\n  -s 150 \\\n  -e 151 \\\n  -t ~/tmp/text-overlays.yaml \\\n  -w 800 \\\n  -f an example.\n  fontFamily:   Sans\n  fontStyle:    Oblique\n  fontStretch:  Normal\n  fontWeight:   30\n  fontSize:     800\n  origin:       NorthWest\n  xTranslation: 0.0\n  yTranslation: 0.0\n  rotation:     20\n  startTime:    5.000\n  endTime:      8.000\n  outlineSize:  7\n  outlineColor: rgba(1,100,10,1.0)\n  fillColor:    rgba(255,255,0,0.0)\n- text: This is another example.\n  fontFamily:   Serif\n  fontStyle:    Italic\n  fontStretch:  Condensed\n  fontWeight:   30\n  fontSize:     800\n  origin:       Center\n  xTranslation: 10.0\n  yTranslation: 11.0\n  rotation:     20\n  startTime:    5.000\n  endTime:      8.000\n  outlineSize:  7\n  outlineColor: rgb(1,11,100)\n  fillColor:    rgba(255,0,0,0.0)\n```\n\n`fontStyle` can be:\n\n- `Any`\n- `Italic`\n- `Normal`\n- `Oblique`\n\n`fontStretch` can be:\n\n- `Any`\n- `Condensed`\n- `Expanded`\n- `ExtraCondensed`\n- `ExtraExpanded`\n- `Normal`\n- `SemiCondensed`\n- `SemiExpanded`\n- `UltraCondensed`\n- `UltraExpanded`\n\n`origin` can be:\n\n- `North`\n- `NorthEast`\n- `East`\n- `SouthEast`\n- `South`\n- `SouthWest`\n- `West`\n- `NorthWest`\n\n`outlineSize` ranges from `0` to `10`.\n\n`outlineColor` and `fillColor` can either be `rgba(red,green,blue,alpha)` or `rgb(red,green,blue)`\nwhere `red`, `green`, and `blue` range from `0` to `255` and `alpha` ranges from `0.0` to `1.0`.\n\n## How do I get a copy of Gifcurry? :floppy_disk:\n\nGifcurry works on Linux, Mac, and most likely Windows.\nMake sure you have FFmpeg, GStreamer, ImageMagick, and GTK+ installed on your machine.\nTo find the latest version of Gifcurry, head over to the\n[releases page](https://github.com/lettier/gifcurry/releases).\n\n### I use Linux. :penguin:\n\nIf you use Linux then the easiest way to grab a copy of Gifcurry of the way\xe2\x80\x94you\'re all set\xe2\x80\x94just double click on the AppImage\nand the GUI will fire right up.\n\nYou can also download and install
