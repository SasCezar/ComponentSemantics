It is stable and production ready, ships\nwith most advanced features from Kimai 1 and many new ones, including but not limited to: \n\nJSON API, invoicing, data exports, multi-timer and punch-in punch-out mode, tagging, multi-user and multi-timezones, \nLDAP and built-in authentication, customizable role permissions, responsive and ready for your mobile device, \nhourly and fixed rates, advanced filtering, money and time budgets with report, support for plugins and many more.\n\n## Installation\n\n- [Recommended setup](https://www.kimai.org/documentation/installation.html#recommended-setup) -
