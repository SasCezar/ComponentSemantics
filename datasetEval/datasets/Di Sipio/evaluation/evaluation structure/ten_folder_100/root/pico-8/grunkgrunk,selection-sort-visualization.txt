b'# Selection sort visualization\r\n![Alt text](sortingaction.gif?raw=true "Start Screen")\r\n\r\nA simple interactive visualization of the selection-sort algorithm: https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Selection_sort.\r\n\r\nI\'m using the pico-8 color palette for the graphics: https://www.romanzolotarev.com/pico-8-color-palette/.\r\n\r\n## How to use\r\nYou will need love2d to use this program. Download here: https://love2d.org/. Once installed just drag and drop \r\nthe visualization folder onto the love2d application. \r\n\r\n## Controls\r\nUse left and right arrow-keys to move the cursor, up and down arrows to increment or decrement the number under the cursor.\r\nPress x at any point to start or stop sorting.\r\n\r\n'
