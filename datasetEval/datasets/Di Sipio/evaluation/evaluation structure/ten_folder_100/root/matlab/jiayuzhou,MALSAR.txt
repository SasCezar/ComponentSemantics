b'MALSAR: Multi-task learning via Structural Regularization\n======\n\n\nThe MALSAR (Multi-tAsk Learning via StructurAl Regularization) package includes the following multi-task learning algorithms:\n\n- Mean-Regularized Multi-Task Learning\n- Multi-Task Learning with Joint Feature Selection\n- Robust Multi-Task Feature Learning\n- Trace-Norm Regularized Multi-Task Learning\n- Alternating Structural Optimization\n- Incoherent Low-Rank and Sparse Learning\n- Robust Low-Rank Multi-Task Learning\n- Clustered Multi-Task Learning\n- Multi-Task Learning with Graph Structures\n- Disease Progression Models\n- Incomplete Multi-Source Fusion (iMSF)\n- Multi-Stage Multi-Source Fusion\n- Multi-Task Clustering\n- Multi-Task Calibration\n\nIf you have any questions regarding MALSAR, please contact Jiayu Zhou at jiayu.zhou@asu.edu.\n'
