https://github.com/coreui/coreui.git\n```\n\n### NPM\n\n``` bash\n$ npm install @coreui/coreui@3.0.0-beta.3 --save\n```\n\n### Yarn\n\n``` bash\n$ yarn add @coreui/coreui@3.0.0-beta.3\n```\n\n### Composer\n\n``` bash\n$ composer require coreui/coreui:3.0.0-beta.3\n```\n\n## Usage\n\n### CSS\n\nCopy-paste the stylesheet `<link>` into your `<head>` before all other stylesheets to load our CSS.\n\n``` html\n<link rel="stylesheet" href="node_modules/@coreui/coreui/dist/css/coreui.min.css">\n```\n\n### JS\n\nMany of our components require the use of JavaScript to function. Specifically, they require [jQuery](https://jquery.com), [Popper.js](https://popper.js.org/), [Bootstrap](https://getbootstrap.com) and our own JavaScript plugins. Place the following `<script>`s near the end of your pages, right before the closing `</body>` tag, to enable them. jQuery must come first, then Popper.js, then Bootstrap, and then our JavaScript plugins.\n\n``` html\n<script src="node_modules/popper.js/dist/umd/popper.min.js"></script>\n<script src="node_modules/@coreui/coreui/dist/js/coreui.bundle.min.js"></script>\n```\n\n## What\'s included\n\nWithin the download you\'ll find the following directories and files, logically grouping common assets and providing both compiled and minified variations.
