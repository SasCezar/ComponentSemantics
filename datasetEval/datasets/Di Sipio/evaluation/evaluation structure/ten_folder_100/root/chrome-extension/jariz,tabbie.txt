b"## RIP Tabbie\n\nHi there,\nTabbie is now officially deprecated.  \nIt does not function anymore because of it's reliance of [rather experimental features in chrome](https://www.polymer-project.org/blog/2015-12-01-deprecating-deep), that have now been completely removed.\n\nWe're working on a rewrite over on [quarryapp/app](https://github.com/quarryapp/app).  \nFollow me [on twitter](https://twitter.com/JariZwarts) to stay up to date on it's progress.  \n\n----\n\n# Tabbie <span style='float:right'>[![Join the chat at https://gitter.im/jariz/tabbie](https://badges.gitter.im/Join%20Chat.svg)](https://gitter.im/jariz/tabbie?utm_source=badge&utm_medium=badge&utm_campaign=pr-badge&utm_content=badge) [![Build Status](https://travis-ci.org/jariz/tabbie.svg?branch=master&style=flat-square)](https://travis-ci.org/jariz/tabbie) [![](https://img.shields.io/badge/Chrome-Extension-yellow.svg?style=flat-square)](https://chrome.google.com/webstore/detail/tabbie/kckhddfnffeofnfjcpdffpeiljicclbd)\n\n![](https://cloud.githubusercontent.com/assets/1415847/7947591/5ebce982-097e-11e5-99b8-2ceb979dfda7.png)\n![](https://cloud.githubusercontent.com/assets/1415847/7947610/806ab334-097e-11e5-91d4-9852390391f3.png)\n![](https://cloud.githubusercontent.com/assets/1415847/7947613/86c0d312-097e-11e5-9c8d-3ce7cfa5361b.png)\n\n### What is it?  \n  \nTabbie keeps you informed, inspired, and up to date through it's beautiful and customizable columns.\nTabbie replaces your 'new tab' page with your favorite websites.\nChoose exactly what content you want to see.\n\n### Content\n\nTabbie has a bunch of build in columns, but **any RSS-complaint site indexed by [Feedly](https://feedly.com) can be added to Tabbie.**\n\nTabbie by default comes with the following services / sites;\n- Dribbble\n- Behance\n- HackerNews\n- Designer News\n- GitHub\n- Reddit (frontpage/multireddit/frontpage of your account)\n- Lobste.rs\n- ProductHunt\n- Gmail\n- PushBullet\n- Codepen\n- Chrome Apps, Bookmarks, and Top sites.\n\n### Features\n- Material design based on Google's design principles.\n- Reposition columns\n- Customizable grid\n- Add/remove columns\n- Customize column-specific settings\n- Decentralized, gets it data straight from 3rd party API's\n- Resize columns\n- Rename columns\n\n\n## Contributing to Tabbie\nWant to add your favorite website? Want to add a awesome column that has nothing to do with websites? You can!  \nIt is extremely simple to contribute to Tabbie! You won't even need to install the extension.\n\n- [Creating your first Tabbie column.](https://github.com/jariz/tabbie/blob/master/CONTRIBUTING.md)\n"
