                                            |\n\n## Usage\n\n### Manual\n\nNavigate to the `lang` folder, download your preferred language files, save them to `/resources/lang/vendor/nova/`.\nFor further details about Nova Localization, please check out [the docs](https://nova.laravel.com/docs/1.0/customization/localization.html).\n\n### Automatic\n\nYou can make sure that you are always up to date with the latest translations by adding two commands on the `pre-update-cmd` hook in your `composer.json` file. Of course, you can do that for as many languages as you want.\n\n```json\n{\n  "scripts": {\n    "pre-update-cmd": [\n      "@php -r \\"mkdir(\'./resources/lang/vendor/nova\', 0776, true);\\"",\n      "@php -r \\"copy(\'https://raw.githubusercontent.com/franzdumfart/laravel-nova-localizations/master/lang/fr.json\', \'./resources/lang/vendor/nova/fr.json\') || exit (1);\\"",\n      "@php -r \\"mkdir(\'./resources/lang/vendor/nova/fr\', 0776, true);\\"",\n      "@php -r \\"copy(\'https://raw.githubusercontent.com/franzdumfart/laravel-nova-localizations/master/lang/fr/validation.php\', \'./resources/lang/vendor/nova/fr/validation.php\') || exit (1);\\""\n    ]\n  }\n}\n```\n\nDon\'t forget to customize for your needs.\n\n### Force Nova localization\n\nIf you need to force the localization of Nova, you can add the following lines to `NovaServiceProvider.php` in the `boot` method :\n\n```php\nNova::serving(function () {\n    App::setLocale(\'fr\');\n});\n```\n\n## Help\n\nFeel free to submit a pull request, add your desired language or updates.\n'
