href="https://beta.observablehq.com/@spencermountain/nouns"><b>Plural/singular:</b></a> - grab the noun-phrases, make em plural:\n```js\ndoc = nlp(\'a bottle of beer on the wall.\')\ndoc.nouns(0).toPlural()\ndoc.out(\'text\')\n//\'The bottles of beer on the wall.\'\n```\n\n* <a href="https://beta.observablehq.com/@spencermountain/compromise-values"><b>Number parsing:</b></a> - parse written-out numbers, and change their form:\n```js\ndoc = nlp(\'ninety five thousand and fifty two\')\ndoc.values().toNumber().out()\n// \'95052\'\n\ndoc = nlp(\'the 23rd of December\')\ndoc.values().add(2).toText()\ndoc.out(\'text\')\n// \'the twenty fifth of December\'\n```\n\n* <a href="https://beta.observablehq.com/@spencermountain/compromise-normalization"><b>Normalization:</b></a> - handle looseness & variety of random text:\n```js\ndoc = nlp("the guest-singer\'s bj\xc3\xb6rk   at seven thirty.").normalize().out(\'text\')\n// \'The guest singer is Bjork at 7:30.\'\n```\n\n* <a href="https://beta.observablehq.com/@spencermountain/verbs"><b>Tense:</b></a> - switch to/from conjugations of any verb\n```js\nlet doc = nlp(\'she sells seashells by the seashore.\')\ndoc.sentences().toFutureTense().out(\'text\')\n//\'she will sell seashells...\'\n\ndoc.verbs().conjugate()\n// [{ PastTense: \'sold\',\n//    Infinitive: \'sell\',\n//    Gerund: \'selling\', ...\n// }]\n```\n\n* <a href="https://github.com/spencermountain/compromise/wiki/Contractions"><b> Contractions:</b></a> - grab, expand and contract:\n```js\ndoc = nlp("we\'re not gonna take it, no we ain\'t gonna take it.")\ndoc.has(\'going\') // true\ndoc.match(\'are not\').length // == 2\ndoc.contractions().expand().out()\n//\'we are not going to take it, no we are not going to take it\'\n```\n\n* <a href="https://beta.observablehq.com/@spencermountain/topics-named-entity-recognition"><b> Named-entities:</b></a> - get the people, places, organizations:\n```js\ndoc = nlp(\'the opera about richard nixon visiting china\')\ndoc.topics().data()\n// [\n//   { text: \'richard nixon\' },\n//   { text: \'china\' }\n// ]\n```\n\n* <a href="https://github.com/spencermountain/compromise/wiki/Lexicon"><b>Custom lexicon:</b></a> - make it do what you\'d like:\n```js\nvar lexicon={\n  \'boston\': \'MusicalGroup\'\n}\ndoc = nlp(\'i heard Boston\\\'s set in Chicago\', lexicon)\n\n//alternatively, fix it \'in-post\':\ndoc.match(\'heard #Possessive set\').terms(1).tag(\'MusicalGroup\')\n```\n\n* <a href="https://beta.observablehq.com/@spencermountain/compromise-output"><b> Handy outputs:</b></a> - get sensible data:\n```js\ndoc = nlp(\'We like Roy! We like Roy!\').sentences().out(\'array\')\n// [\'We like Roy!\', \'We like Roy!\']\n\ndoc = nlp(\'Tony Hawk\').out(\'html\')\n/*\n<span>\n  <span class="nl-Person nl-FirstName">Tony</span>\n  <span>&nbsp;</span>\n  <span class="nl-Person nl-LastName">Hawk</span>\n</span>\n*/\n```\n\n<!-- plugins section -->\n* <a href="https://beta.observablehq.com/@spencermountain/compromise-plugins"><b> Plugins:</b></a> - allow adding vocabulary, fixing errors, and setting context quickly:\n```js\nvar plugin = {\n  tags:{\n    Character:{\n      isA: \'Noun\'\n    }\n  },\n  words:{\n    itchy: \'Character\',\n    scratchy: \'Character\'\n  }\n}\nnlp.plugin(plugin)\nnlp(`Couldn\'t Itchy share his pie with Scratchy?`).debug()\n/*\n   couldn\'t   - #Modal, #Verb\n   itchy      - #Character, #Noun\n   share      - #Infinitive, #Verb\n   ...\n*/\n```\n\n<h3 align="center">\n  of course, there\'s <a href="https://beta.observablehq.com/@spencermountain/nlp-compromise">a lot more
