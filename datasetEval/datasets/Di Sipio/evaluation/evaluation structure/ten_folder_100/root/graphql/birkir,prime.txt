commit](https://img.shields.io/github/last-commit/google/skia.svg)\n[![license](https://img.shields.io/github/license/birkir/prime.svg)](https://opensource.org/licenses/MIT)\n\nPrime is a standalone, self-hosted, headless CMS with a GraphQL interface powered by TypeScript.\n\n## Why Prime?\n\nThere are a lot of headless SaaS solutions out there, and many of them better than Prime, but sometimes there is a need to host the CMS locally and while most open source projects work well for that, they are missing key features.\n\n> Please don\xe2\x80\x99t forget to star this repo if you found it useful\n\n## Features\n\n- \xf0\x9f\x96\xa8 Headless GraphQL Interface\n- \xf0\x9f\x9a\x80 Simple to use, yet powerful\n- \xf0\x9f\x93\x90 Slices and Groups fields\n- \xe2\x98\x91\xef\xb8\x8f Create your own custom fields\n- \xf0\x9f\x87\xae\xf0\x9f\x87\xb8 \xf0\x9f\x87\xaf\xf0\x9f\x87\xb5 Multiple languages\n- \xf0\x9f\x9a\xa7 Preview drafts and releases without publishing\n- \xf0\x9f\x94\x91 Resource and user access control\n- \xf0\x9f\x93\x86 Plan and schedule releases\n- ... and many more\n\nSee the [feature comparison](https://docs.primecms.app/#/features) to Prismic, Contentful and Strapi.\n\n## Examples\n\nIntegration examples with previews.\n\n- [Gatsby](https://github.com/primecms/example-gatsby) (React)\n- [Next](https://github.com/primecms/example-nextjs)
