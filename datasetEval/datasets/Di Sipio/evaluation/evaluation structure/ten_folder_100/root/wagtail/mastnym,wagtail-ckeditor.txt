install\n----\nInclude `wagtail_ckeditor` in your `INSTALLED_APPS`.\n\nEnsure that you have this entry in your `settings.py` file.\n\n\n.. code-block:: python\n\n    WAGTAILADMIN_RICH_TEXT_EDITORS = {\n        \'default\': {\n            \'WIDGET\': \'wagtail_ckeditor.widgets.CKEditor\'\n        }\n    }\n\nThere are several options you can add to your `settings.py` file.\n\n- Use Mathjax plugin to be able to insert mathematical formulas\n\n        WAGTAIL_CKEDITOR_USE_MATH = True\n- Uf above set to true you need to specify an url to Mathjax Library, defaults to:\n\n        WAGTAIL_CKEDITOR_MATHJAX_URL = getattr(settings, \'WAGTAIL_CKEDITOR_MATHJAX_URL\', "//cdnjs.cloudflare.com/ajax/libs/mathjax/2.7.0/MathJax.js?config=TeX-AMS_HTML")\n- CKEditor settings, defaults to:\n
