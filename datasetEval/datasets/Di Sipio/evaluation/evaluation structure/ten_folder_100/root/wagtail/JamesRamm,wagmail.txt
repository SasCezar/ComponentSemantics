b"Wagmail\n=============================\n\nCreate, send and schedule emails from the Wagtail admin.\n\nThis project is in the very early stages of development and not yet ready for use!\n\nThe overall aims of this project are to do the following from the Wagtail admin:\n\n* Allow emails to be written and sent to registered users\n* Allow email templates (i.e. django templates) to be created\n* Allow templates to be rendered and sent automatically on specific signals to\n    - all users\n    - predefined selection of users\n    - email address' passed in via custom receivers/hooks\n\n\nEmail Templates\n---------------\n\n- Allow templates to be written in rich text in the admin.\n"
