b"# Racket mode for GNU Emacs\n\n[![Build Status](https://travis-ci.org/greghendershott/racket-mode.svg?branch=master)](https://travis-ci.org/greghendershott/racket-mode)\n[![MELPA](https://melpa.org/packages/racket-mode-badge.svg)](https://melpa.org/#/racket-mode)\n[![Documentation](https://img.shields.io/badge/Docs-Documentation-blue.svg)](https://www.racket-mode.com/)\n\nThis provides a major mode to edit [Racket] source files, as well as a\nmajor mode for a Racket REPL. The edit/run experience is similar to\n[DrRacket].\n\n[Racket]: https://www.racket-lang.org/\n[DrRacket]: https://docs.racket-lang.org/drracket/\n\nCompatible with **Emacs 24.3+** and **Racket 6.2+**.\n\n## Documentation\n\nSee the [Guide and Reference](https://www.racket-mode.com/).\n\n## Contributing\n\nPull requests are welcome; please [read this](CONTRIBUTING.md).\n\n[Acknowledgments](THANKS.md).\n\n## Alternatives\n\n- Emacs' built-in `scheme-mode` major mode plus the minor modes [Quack]\n  and/or [Geiser].\n\n[Quack]: https://www.neilvandyke.org/quack/\n[Geiser]: https://www.nongnu.org/geiser/\n"
