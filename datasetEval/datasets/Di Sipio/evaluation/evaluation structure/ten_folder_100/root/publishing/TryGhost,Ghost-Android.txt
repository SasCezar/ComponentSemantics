Ideal for writing on the go\n- **Attach tags and a cover image** - upload images from your phone or a web link\n- **[Conflict handling][conflict-ui]** - a simple UI to help decide what to do next when a post is edited from two places at once\n\n### Bug reports? Feature requests?\n\n[File an issue](/CONTRIBUTING.md)\n\n### Help translate Ghost Android\n\nIf you\'d like to see support for your language in Ghost Android, you can easily [contribute translations on Weblate][weblate]. _This requires no setup and no knowledge of Android development_, just keep in mind a few simple rules:\n\n- Many strings have [placeholders](http://envyandroid.com/android-string-xml-resource-formatting/) like "%s", "%d", "%2$s", etc. - keep these intact because other numbers and strings are inserted into these placeholders\n- Single and double quotes need to be preceded with a backslash character (`\\\'` and `\\"` respectively)\n- For short strings, try to keep the translated string length close to the English one, because longer strings may not fit in the UI\n\nIf you need help getting started, drop a comment on [this issue](https://github.com/TryGhost/Ghost-Android/issues/14).\n\n### Developer setup\n\nSetup is as simple as importing the project into Android Studio and building (assuming you have the correct build tools and Android SDK).\n\nIf you face any issues setting this up, please let
