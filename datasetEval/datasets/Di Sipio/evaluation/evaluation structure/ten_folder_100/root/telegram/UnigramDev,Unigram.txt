Store](#alternativedownload)\n- [Wiki](#wiki)\n\n\n<a name="features"></a>\n## Features\n- Based on [TDLib](https://github.com/tdlib/td)\n- Secret chats\n- Instant view (now updated with the most recent 2.0)\n- Multi-account\n- Filter chats (users, bots, groups, channels, unread chats and unmuted chats)\n- Calls\n- Videos and photos editor (easily crop photos, set video resultion or remove some parts of the videos before sending them)\n- Drag&drop files to chats to send them quickly\n- Open chats in separated windows (Shift+click)\n- Replace sent media content\n- Polls\n- Backgrounds 2.0 (blurred and motion options available)\n- Built-in app lock (fingerprint and Windows Hello authentication available)\n- Windows My People integration\n- Full support of Windows 10 notifications and Action Centre (reply to a message or mark it as read without opening the app)\n- Accessibility for visually impaired people\n\n\n<a name="contributing"></a>\n## Contributing\nBefore reporting
