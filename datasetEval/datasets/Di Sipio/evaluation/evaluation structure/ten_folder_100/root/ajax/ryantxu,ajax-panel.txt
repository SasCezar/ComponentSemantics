b"## AJAX Panel for Grafana\n\n[![CircleCI](https://circleci.com/gh/ryantxu/ajax-panel/tree/master.svg?style=svg)](https://circleci.com/gh/ryantxu/ajax-panel/tree/master)\n[![dependencies Status](https://david-dm.org/ryantxu/ajax-panel/status.svg)](https://david-dm.org/ryantxu/ajax-panel)\n[![devDependencies Status](https://david-dm.org/ryantxu/ajax-panel/dev-status.svg)](https://david-dm.org/ryantxu/ajax-panel?type=dev)\n\nThe AJAX Panel is a general way to load external content into a grafana dashboard.\n\n### Options\n\n* **Method**:\n\n  GET or POST or iframe\n\n* **URL**:\n\n  The URL to request\n\n* **Parameters**:\n\n  The parameters that will be passed in the request.
