& Facade (Optional on Laravel 5.5)\nRegister provider and facade on your `config/app.php` file.\n```php\n\'providers\' => [\n    ...,\n    Yajra\\DataTables\\DataTablesServiceProvider::class,\n]\n\n\'aliases\' => [\n    ...,\n    \'DataTables\' => Yajra\\DataTables\\Facades\\DataTables::class,\n]\n```\n\n#### Configuration (Optional)\n```bash\n$ php artisan vendor:publish --provider="Yajra\\DataTables\\DataTablesServiceProvider"\n```\n\nAnd that\'s it!
