Running `nmap` without any parameters will give a helpful\nlist of the most common options, which are discussed in depth in [the man\npage](https://nmap.org/book/man.html). Users who prefer a graphical interface\ncan use the included [Zenmap front-end](https://nmap.org/zenmap/).\n\nContributing\n------------\nInformation about filing bug reports and contributing to the Nmap project can\nbe found in the [HACKING](HACKING) and [CONTRIBUTING.md](CONTRIBUTING.md)\nfiles.\n'
