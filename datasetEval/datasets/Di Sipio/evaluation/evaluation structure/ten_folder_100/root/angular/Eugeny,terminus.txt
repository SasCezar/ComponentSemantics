Neither is it lightweight - if RAM usage is of importance, consider [Conemu](https://conemu.github.io) or [Alacritty](https://github.com/jwilm/alacritty)\n\n---\n\n# Plugins\n\nPlugins and themes can be installed directly from the Settings view inside Terminus.\n\n  * [clickable-links](https://github.com/Eugeny/terminus-clickable-links) - makes paths and URLs in the terminal clickable\n  * [shell-selector](https://github.com/Eugeny/terminus-shell-selector) - a quick shell selector pane\n  * [title-control](https://github.com/kbjr/terminus-title-control) - allows modifying the title of the terminal tabs by providing a prefix, suffix, and/or strings to be removed\n  * [quick-cmds](https://github.com/Domain/terminus-quick-cmds) - quickly send commands to one or all terminal tabs\n  * [save-output](https://github.com/Eugeny/terminus-save-output) - record terminal output into a file\n  * [scrollbar](https://github.com/kbjr/terminus-scrollbar) - adds a scrollbar to hterm tabs\n\n# Themes\n\n  * [hype](https://github.com/Eugeny/terminus-theme-hype) - a Hyper inspired theme\n  * [relaxed](https://github.com/Relaxed-Theme/relaxed-terminal-themes#terminus) - the Relaxed theme for Terminus\n  * [gruvbox](https://github.com/porkloin/terminus-theme-gruvbox)\n  * [windows10](https://www.npmjs.com/package/terminus-theme-windows10)\n  * [altair](https://github.com/yxuko/terminus-altair)\n\n---\n\n# Contributing\n\nPull requests and plugins are welcome!\n\nSee [HACKING.md](https://github.com/Eugeny/terminus/blob/master/HACKING.md) and
