[Node.js](https://nodejs.org) v8.11 or greater\n- [Gulp](https://gulpjs.com/) (`npm install --global gulp-cli`)\n- Chrome (preferred), Opera, or Firefox browser\n\n**Run the app**\n\n```\nnpm install\nnpm run build:watch\n```\n\nYou can now load the extension into your browser through the browser\'s extension tools page:\n\n- Chrome/Opera:\n  1. Type `chrome://extensions` in your address bar to bring up the extensions page.\n  2. Enable developer mode (checkbox)\n  3. Click the "Load unpacked extension" button, navigate to the `build` folder of your local extension instance, and click "Ok".\n- Firefox\n  1. Type `about:debugging` in your address bar to bring up the add-ons page.\n  2. Click the `Load Temporary Add-on` button, navigate to the `build/manifest.json` file, and "Open".\n\n# Contribute\n\nCode contributions are welcome!
