b"[![Build Status](https://travis-ci.org/mhinz/vim-startify.svg?branch=master)](https://travis-ci.org/mhinz/vim-startify)\n\n![vim-startify](https://raw.githubusercontent.com/mhinz/vim-startify/master/images/startify-logo.png)\n\n---\n\nThis plugin provides a start screen for Vim and Neovim.\n\nIt provides **dynamically created headers or footers** and uses configurable\nlists to show **recently used or bookmarked files** and **persistent sessions**.\nAll of this can be accessed in a **simple to use menu** that even allows to\n**open multiple entries** at once.\n\nStartify doesn't get in your way and works out-of-the-box, but provides many\noptions for fine-grained customization.\n\n---\n\n- [Installation & Documentation](#installation-and-documentation)\n- [Plugin features in detail](https://github.com/mhinz/vim-startify/wiki/Plugin-features-in-detail)\n- [Screenshot](#screenshot)\n- [Author & Feedback](#author-and-feedback)\n\n---\n\n## Installation and Documentation\n\nUse your favorite plugin manager.\n\nUsing [vim-plug](https://github.com/junegunn/vim-plug):\n\n    Plug 'mhinz/vim-startify'\n\nIt works without any configuration, but you might want to look into the\ndocumentation for further customization:\n\n    :h startify\n    :h startify-faq\n\n## Screenshot\n\n![Startify in action!](https://github.com/mhinz/vim-startify/blob/master/images/startify-menu.png)\n\nThat's it.
