b"# Traduora\n[![Latest Release](https://img.shields.io/github/release/traduora/traduora.svg?label=latest%20release)](https://github.com/traduora/traduora/releases)\n[![GitHub License](https://img.shields.io/badge/license-AGPL-v3.svg)](https://raw.githubusercontent.com/traduora/traduora/master/LICENSE)\n[![CircleCI](https://circleci.com/gh/traduora/traduora.svg?style=svg)](https://circleci.com/gh/traduora/traduora)\n[![Docker Pulls](https://img.shields.io/docker/pulls/traduora/traduora)](https://hub.docker.com/r/traduora/traduora)\n\nTraduora is a **translation management platform for teams**. Once you setup your project you can import and export your translations to various formats, work together with your team, instantly deliver translation updates over the air, and *soon* automatically translate your project via third-party integrations.\n\nWe want traduora to become the home for managing your translation workflow, that's why we have made all of the core product **open-source** with the intention to grow a **community** and enable developers to build on top of it as a platform.\n\n![Traduora Product Screenshot](docs-website/static/img/traduora-preview.png)\n\n\n## Features\n- 5 minute setup with Docker, Kubernetes or from source\n- Find what you are looking for with instant search\n- Invite your team, everyone can work together on the same project\n- Automate your translation workflow via our REST API\n- Import and export to your favourite formats: JSON flat and nested, CSV, YAML flat and nested, Java Properties, XLIFF 1.2, Gettext (po), Strings and more coming soon!\n- *Coming soon:* Web and Mobile SDKs\n- *Coming soon:* Automatic translations\n\nFor more information check out [traduora.com](https://traduora.com), or our docs at [docs.traduora.com](https://docs.traduora.com)\n\nAny missing feature you'd like to see? File an [issue](https://github.com/traduora/traduora/issues) with the feature request to let us know.\n\n\n## Try it out\nTraduora can be run just about anywhere, check out our [quickstart](https://docs.traduora.com/docs/getting-started) for more info.\n\nAlso check out traduora's [Docker Hub page](https://hub.docker.com/r/traduora/traduora) for pre-built images.\n\n\n### Configuration and Deployment\nPlease check out the [configuration](https://docs.traduora.com/docs/configuration) and [deployment](https://docs.traduora.com/docs/deployment) documents for more information on deploying traduora.\n\n\n## Frequently Asked Questions\nSome questions come up over and over again.
