Legend has it that the sword bearer becomes enlightened\nin the JavaScript language, but be warned: the journey will not be easy. On each\nfloor, you need to write JavaScript to instruct the warrior to battle enemies,\nrescue captives, and reach the stairs alive...\n\n**No matter if you are new to programming or a JavaScript guru, WarriorJS will\nput your skills to the test. Will you dare?**\n\n## Play\n\nGo to [warriorjs.com](https://warriorjs.com/?ref=gh) and play from the comfort\nof your browser! Sharpen your skills and compete against other players around\nthe globe. Good luck in your journey, warrior!\n\n## Documentation\n\nAlthough there is some in-game documentation, at some point you may want to\nvisit the [official docs](https://warrior.js.org).\n\nJump straight to some of the most-visited pages:\n\n- [Gameplay](https://warrior.js.org/docs/player/gameplay)\n- [Towers](https://warrior.js.org/docs/player/towers)\n- [Player API](https://warrior.js.org/docs/player/space-api)\n\n## CLI\n\nWanna play offline? Launch the game:\n\n```sh\nwarriorjs\n```\n\n3.  Create your warrior.\n\n4.  You\'ll be pointed to a README file with instructions for the first level.\n\nCheck out the [Install](https://warrior.js.org/docs/player/install) docs for\nmore details.\n\n## Preview\n\n![WarriorJS Preview](https://user-images.githubusercontent.com/5600126/40150855-8b2acdd2-5952-11e8-9034-00dcee0c4c2c.gif)\n\n> WarriorJS CLI launched from the\n> [Integrated Terminal](https://code.visualstudio.com/docs/editor/integrated-terminal)\n> in [VS Code](https://code.visualstudio.com).
