b'Kaishi\n======\n\nKaishi is a bash script to set up your Mac OSX for full web stack Rails development.\n\n\nRequirements\n------------\n\nInstall the C compiler:\n\nFor Lion (10.7) or Mountain Lion (10.8) use [Command Line Tools for Xcode](https://developer.apple.com/downloads/index.action)\n\nFor Mavericks (10.9) or Yosemite (10.10), execute `sudo xcodebuild -license` and follow the instructions to accept the XCode agreement. After that you just run `xcode-select --install` and proceed with the installation.\n\n\nInstallation\n------------\n\nRemember, with great power comes great responsibility!\n\n### Mac OSX\n\n\tbash <(curl -s https://raw.githubusercontent.com/IcaliaLabs/kaishi/master/kaishi-mac)\n\t\n### Linux\n\t\n\twget https://raw.githubusercontent.com/IcaliaLabs/kaishi/master/kaishi-linux -v -O kaishi.sh && bash kaishi.sh; rm -f kaishi.sh\n\t\nAnd that is it!\n\n\nSo what it\'s in the box\n-----------------------\n\n### Mac OSX\n\n* [oh-my-zsh](https://github.com/robbyrussell/oh-my-zsh) as your default
