In other words, it\'s a **Danger As A Service**. The differences of original danger are "when" and "what" to be executed.\n\n- :heavy_check_mark: **When**: Hosted Danger is detached from CI processes. So danger can be executed interactively. (It\'s reacted for Github Webhooks.)\n- :heavy_check_mark: **What**: It cannot refer the source codes and build products basically. It\'s specialized for Pull Requests automations, not for showing results of lints, tests or coverages.\n\n:rocket: The Hosted Danger is activated on **more than 1500 repositoies** in Yahoo! JAPAN. :rocket:\n\n<i>[danger/danger](https://github.com/danger/danger) is really awesome OSS.
