b'# CryptoCurrency\nPage to keep track of value & profits of a portfolio of cryptocurrency (based on Coinmarketcap, Bitfinex and Binance)\n\nFeatures:\n- Login screen, no database required\n- A tab to keep track of your paper wallet portfolio\n- A tab to keep track of your Bitfinex portfolio\n- A tab to keep track of your Binance portfolio\n- A tab to keep track of potential coins with simulated profit\n- A summary tab listing profits per paper wallet/exchange only\n- Easy to add interesting coins if necessary\n\nCoins for paper wallet, using public keys:\n- Bitcoin\n- Ethereum\n- Litecoin\n- WePower (technically a token but yeah)\n\nCoins for exchanges, manual entry:\n- Bitcoin\n- Bitcoin cash\n- Cardano\n- Ethereum Classic\n- Iota\n- Litecoin\n- Monero\n- Neo\n- Ripple\n- VeChain\n\nCoins for watchlist:\n- Burst\n- Eos\n- Verge\n- Waves\n- Zcash\n\nHow to use:\n\nInput.php can be used to add:\n- Public key of owned coins/tokens, 1 per coin only. Limited to BTC, ETH, LTC and WPR\n- Buyin rate: value of 1 of each coin at moment of buying, in dollars\n- Buyin costs: Amount of money spent on purchase per coin, in dollars\n- Transferfee: The usual transfer fee in the respective coin, not dollars\n- Withdrawalfee: The withdrawal fee for the coin at the specific exchange in the respective coin, not dollars\n\nCoins.php shows basic information on:\n- Actual rate of a coin according to Coinmarketcap\n- Recent percentage changes according to Coinmarketcap per 1 hour / 24 hours / 7 days\n- Profit (absolute and percentage)\n- Profit deducting regular fees (absolute and percentage)\n\nDependencies:\n- Coins.php uses exchange rates for EUR/USD from OpenExchangeRates.org. You need to sign up with them at0 https://openexchangerates.org for an API-key\n- Coins.php uses cryptocurrency exchange rates and percentage changes from Coinmarketcap that are updated every 5 minutes\n- Balance.php uses Blockchain.info, Etherscan.io and Chainz.cryptoid.info to fetch balance data for public adresses\n- Bitfinex.php uses Bitfinex API to fetch data for rates of coins at Bitfinex\n- Binance.php uses Binance API to fetch data for rates of coins at Bitfinex\n\nPlan to change:\n- Add actual fees (if any site supplies this through an API)\n- Add ATH (all time high and 2/3 of ATH as info if any site supplies this through an API)\n'
