b'# Ember Power Calendar [![Build Status](https://travis-ci.org/cibernox/ember-power-calendar.svg?branch=master)](https://travis-ci.org/cibernox/ember-power-calendar)\n\nCustomizable Calendar Component for Ember.\n\n## Disclaimer\n\nVersion 0.14 of this addon requires Ember 3.11 or greater.\nVersions below 0.14 would work with Ember 3.0+ (and perhaps 2.12+)\n\n## Installation\n\n`ember install ember-power-calendar`\n\nInternet Explorer 11 support requires babel polyfill:\n\n```js\n// ember-cli-build.js\n\nlet app = new EmberAddon(defaults, {\n  \'ember-cli-babel\': {\n    includePolyfill: true\n  }\n});\n```\n\n## Usage\n\nThere are many possible ways to use it, for giving you just a taste of the API:\n\n```hbs\n<PowerCalendar @selected={{arrival}} @onSelect={{action (mut arrival) value="date"}} as |calendar|>\n  <calendar.Nav/>\n  <calendar.Days/>\n</PowerCalendar>\n```\n\nCheck the full documentation at www.ember-power-calendar.com\n\n'
