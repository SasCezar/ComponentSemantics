through queries in GraphQL, example:\n\n```javascript\n{\n  pokemon(name: "Pikachu") {\n    id\n    number\n    name\n    attacks {\n      special {\n        name\n        type\n        damage\n      }\n    }\n    evolutions {\n      id\n      number\n      name\n      weight {\n        minimum\n        maximum\n      }\n      attacks {\n        fast {\n          name\n          type\n          damage\n        }\n      }\n    }\n  }\n}\n```\n\n> Try this query
