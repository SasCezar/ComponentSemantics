    "name": "Ruuvi 1",\n      "id": "ca67bf52ca12"\n    },\n    {\n      "accessory": "Ruuvitag",\n      "name": "Ruuvi 2",\n      "id": "fa81b4c6a891"\n    },\n    {\n      "accessory": "Ruuvitag",\n      "name": "Ruuvi 3",\n      "id": "ac67df12bb34"\n    }\n  ]\n}\n```\n\n## Run\n\nNow you can run Homebridge:\n```bash\nhomebridge\n```\n\n## Start on startup\n\nInstall pm2:\n```bash\nnpm -g i
