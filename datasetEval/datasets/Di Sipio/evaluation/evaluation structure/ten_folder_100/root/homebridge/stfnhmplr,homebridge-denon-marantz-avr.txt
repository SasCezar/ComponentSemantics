b'# Homebridge-Denon-Marantz-AVR\n\nhomebridge-plugin for Denon and Marantz AVR control with Apple-Homekit. Works with most Denon AVR since 2011, supports a second zone and implements the speaker service.\n\n# Installation\nFollow the instruction in [NPM](https://www.npmjs.com/package/homebridge) for the homebridge server installation. The plugin is published through [NPM](https://www.npmjs.com/package/homebridge-denon) and should be installed "globally" by typing:\n\n    sudo npm install -g homebridge-denon\n\n# Configuration\n\nconfig.json\n\nExample:\n```\n{\n    "bridge": {\n        "name": "Homebridge",\n        "username": "CC:22:3D:E3:CE:30",\n        "port": 51826,\n        "pin": "123-45-678"\n    },\n\n    "description": "just an example config",\n\n    "platforms": [\n        {\n          "platform": "DenonMarantzAVR",\n          "name": "Denon LivingRoom",\n          "host": "192.168.178.85",\n          "maxVolume": 70,\n          "secondZone": true\n        }\n    ]\n}\n```\n\n### notes\nIf you are interested in setting the volume of your receiver with Siri than [this](https://github.com/robertvorthman/homebridge-marantz-volume) plugin might be a good addition. Only remember to not tell Siri "Set the light in the Living room to 100 %" ;)\nhomebridge-marantz-volume was written by Robert Vorthman (thanks!)'
