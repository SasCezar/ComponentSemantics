b'# homebridge-awair\nAwair plugin for homebridge: https://github.com/nfarina/homebridge\n\nBased on the great work of [@henrypoydar](https://github.com/henrypoydar).\n\nThis is a very basic plugin for Nfarina\'s amazing [Homebridge project](https://github.com/nfarina/homebridge). It will fetch current sensor conditions from an Awair device (e.g. Awair, Awair Glow, Awair Mint, Awair Omni, or Awair 2nd Edition) and provide available sensor readings (e.g. temperature, humidity, carbon dioxide, TVOC, and dust/PM2.5/PM10) information for HomeKit.\n\nYou can look at the current Awair information via HomeKit enabled Apps on your iOS device or even ask Siri for them.\n\nIt will get new data once every 15 minutes (default), but it can be customized in `config.json`.\n\n# Installation\n\n1.
